## Handicap System

The handicap numbers (HCP column) indicate the difficulty ranking of each hole:
- **1** = Hardest hole (Hole 14 - Par 5, 548 yards from Blue)
- **18** = Easiest hole (Hole 8 - Par 3, 149 yards from Blue)

Handicap allocation is based on traditional stroke allocation system, considering hole difficulty, length, and scoring averages.
