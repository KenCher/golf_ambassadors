# 📱 Group Score Entry Guide

## Overview
Players can enter scores for their entire foursome (group) while playing, not just their own scores. This makes it easy for one person in the group to track everyone's scores in real-time.

## 🎯 How to Use Group Score Entry

### **Step 1: Access the Score Entry Page**
Players should use the **Admin Score Entry Link** provided when the event was created:
```
https://golf-ambassadors.web.app/golf-ambassadors.html?event=YOUR_EVENT_ID
```

### **Step 2: Select Your Name**
1. Open the score entry page
2. Select your name from the "Select Your Name" dropdown
3. This identifies you as the person entering scores

### **Step 3: Switch to Group Mode**
1. Look for the mode selector buttons at the top:
   - **👤 My Scores Only** (default)
   - **👥 Group Scores (Foursome)**
2. Click **"👥 Group Scores (Foursome)"**

### **Step 4: Select Your Group Members**
1. After switching to Group mode, a player selection section appears
2. Check the boxes next to the players in your foursome (up to 4 players including yourself)
3. You'll see: "X players selected for group entry"

### **Step 5: Enter Scores**
1. The scorecard now shows columns for each selected player
2. Enter scores hole-by-hole for all players in your group
3. Scores are color-coded by player for easy identification
4. Stableford points are calculated automatically

### **Step 6: Save Scores**
1. Click **"💾 Save All Scores"** button
2. Scores are saved for all selected players at once
3. Scores sync to Firebase automatically

## 🔄 Switching Between Modes

### **My Scores Only Mode**
- Enter scores only for yourself
- Simpler interface
- Good for individual play

### **Group Scores Mode**
- Enter scores for up to 4 players
- Side-by-side score entry
- Perfect for foursomes

You can switch between modes at any time!

## 💡 Best Practices

### **Who Should Enter Scores?**
- Designate one person per foursome to enter all scores
- Usually the player with the best phone/signal
- Can rotate the responsibility each hole if desired

### **When to Enter Scores?**
- **Recommended**: Enter after each hole while walking to the next tee
- **Alternative**: Enter every 3 holes during breaks
- **Not Recommended**: Waiting until the end (easy to forget scores)

### **What If Multiple People Enter?**
- Last save wins (most recent scores are kept)
- Coordinate within your group to avoid conflicts
- One designated scorer per group works best

## 📱 Mobile Tips

### **For Best Experience:**
1. Add the page to your home screen (PWA)
2. Keep phone charged (bring portable charger)
3. Use landscape mode for easier entry
4. Enable auto-brightness for outdoor visibility

### **If Connection is Poor:**
- Scores save locally first
- Will sync to cloud when connection improves
- Don't worry about losing data

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### **Can't See Group Mode Button?**
- Make sure you've selected your name first
- Refresh the page
- Check that you're using the correct event link

### **Players Not Showing in Selection?**
- Verify players are registered for the event
- Check that event is selected in dropdown
- Contact admin to add missing players

### **Scores Not Saving?**
- Check internet connection
- Verify you clicked "Save All Scores"
- Scores save locally even without internet

## 🎯 Example Workflow

**John's Foursome:**
1. John, Mike, Sarah, and Tom are playing together
2. John opens the score entry link on his phone
3. John selects his name: "John Smith"
4. John clicks "👥 Group Scores (Foursome)"
5. John checks boxes for: John, Mike, Sarah, Tom
6. After Hole 1, John enters all four scores
7. John clicks "Save All Scores"
8. Repeat for each hole
9. Everyone's scores appear on the live leaderboard!
